  • What To Do With Robux?

    For 100 ROBUX, users in a premium membership can create groups. For 500 ROBUX, group owners can create a clan for their group. For 1,000 ROBUX, users can change their username. Users can upload thumbnails for their places. An image thumbnail requires 10 ROBUX, while a video thumbnail requires 500 ROBUX. Users can purchase items in the catalog for varying amounts of ROBUX. Users can bid for an advertisement for their games, clothing, groups or models for different amounts of robux. Players can buy game passes and developer products in different games. Users can create a new rank for their group for 25 R$ each. Users can upload an audio for 60 R$. Price depends on how long the audio is. ROBUX can be used for game passes and gears in games.

What Is Robux?

Robux are the primary currency for Roblox. Members of the Builders Club receive a daily Robux stipend. Builders Club members also can sell shirts, pants and place access and receive 70% of the profit. You can purchase Robux on the Robux page. Both Builders Club and Non Builders Club members can sell game passes for Robux. Non-BC members will get 10% of the profit while BC members will get 70% of the profit. The Builders Club and Non Builders Club members can participate in the Affiliate Program.
